Our Story

How two quiet souls found the same song.

They met one late autumn in a little bookshop in Paris — he was reaching for a Neruda, she was already holding it. A shared smile, a shared coffee, and a conversation that slipped past the closing hour.

Seasons turned. Letters were written. Cities were crossed. And under a canopy of winter stars, between two small silver rings, a promise quietly became forever.
